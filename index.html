FEATURED WORK

Industrial Compliance Tool

Safety Complain

A web‑based reporting tool designed to capture workplace safety issues directly from users. It provides a structured way for employees or stakeholders to submit hazard complaints in real time, ensuring that incidents are documented quickly and consistently. The form is hosted online, making it accessible from any device without requiring installation, and it integrates fields for describing hazards, categorizing risks, and triggering follow‑up actions. In essence, it’s a digital entry point for a broader safety management system, helping organizations streamline compliance, track interventions, and promote a culture of proactive risk reporting.

Interactive Games

Shake Run

A web‑based motion game that runs directly in the browser, designed to turn a smartphone into a controller by using its accelerometer and gyroscope sensors. Instead of tapping buttons, players move their avatar by physically shaking or tilting their device, creating a fast‑paced and highly interactive racing experience. With a responsive HTML/CSS layout, custom fonts, and JavaScript handling real‑time motion events, the game blends accessibility and excitement—requiring no installation, yet delivering competitive, immersive gameplay that connects physical movement with digital racing fun.

Digital Safety Management

Permit To Work

A comprehensive digital document designed to standardize and control the process of requesting and authorizing work permits in industrial or operational environments. It gathers detailed information such as the safety supervisor’s name and ID, the responsible unit, the person in charge of the job, the exact work location, and a clear description of the task, along with the submission date. By requiring these fields, the form ensures that every permit request is properly documented, traceable, and aligned with safety compliance standards. Its structured layout not only helps supervisors evaluate risks and responsibilities before work begins, but also creates a centralized record that supports accountability, regulatory audits, and operational transparency. In practice, this form functions as both a safety checkpoint and an administrative log, bridging communication between management, safety officers, and workers to reduce hazards and maintain consistent oversight across multiple job sites..
